# Hardware Requirements

To effectively participate in this course and run the simulations and exercises, the following hardware specifications are recommended:

## Minimum Specifications
*   **Processor:** Quad-core CPU (Intel i5/AMD Ryzen 5 or equivalent)
*   **RAM:** 16 GB DDR4
*   **Graphics Card:** NVIDIA GPU with at least 8 GB VRAM (e.g., NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 or equivalent). CUDA compute capability 7.0+.
*   **Storage:** 250 GB SSD (Solid State Drive)
*   **Operating System:** Ubuntu 20.04 LTS (recommended) or Windows 10/11 with WSL2.

## Recommended Specifications
*   **Processor:** Hexa-core CPU (Intel i7/AMD Ryzen 7 or equivalent)
*   **RAM:** 32 GB DDR4 or more
*   **Graphics Card:** NVIDIA GPU with at least 12 GB VRAM (e.g., NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3070/3080 or equivalent). CUDA compute capability 7.5+.
*   **Storage:** 500 GB NVMe SSD
*   **Operating System:** Ubuntu 22.04 LTS

## Edge AI Kits (Optional)
*   NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano/Xavier NX Developer Kit for deploying ROS 2 and basic AI models.
*   Raspberry Pi 4 (8GB) for basic ROS 2 experimentation.

## Robot Lab Options (Optional)
*   Access to a Unitree Go1/Go2 or similar quadruped robot for real-world deployment (beyond the scope of required exercises but beneficial for advanced learners).
